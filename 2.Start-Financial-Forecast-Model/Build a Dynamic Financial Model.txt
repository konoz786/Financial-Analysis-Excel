Formulas :

1.Number of orders = Previous Number of Order(1+Order Growth rate)

2.Revenue =Number of Orders * Average Order Value

3.Gross Profit =Revenue - total cost sold

4.Gross Profit margin = Gross Profit/Revenue

5.Operating Profit = Revenue -(Total operating Expenses+ Total Cost of Product Sold)

6.Operating Profit margin = Operating profit / Revenue 

7.Corporate Tax = Corporate Tax rate* Profit
