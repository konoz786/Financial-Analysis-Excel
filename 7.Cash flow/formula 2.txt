┌───────────────────────────────────────────┐
│           FREE CASH FLOW (FCF)            │
├───────────────────────────────────────────┤
│                                           │
│ FCF = EBIT                                │
│      - Taxes                              │
│      + Depreciation & Amortization        │
│      - Capital Expenditures (CapEx)       │
│      - Δ Non-Cash Working Capital         │
│                                           │
└───────────────────────────────────────────┘
WHERE:
• Δ = Change in (Current Period - Prior Period)
• Non-Cash Working Capital = 
    (Current Assets - Cash) - (Current Liabilities - Debt)
